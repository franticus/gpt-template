Cookies Policy


CricWinnerTop is a daily fantasy esports betting application. This page pertains exclusively to the CricWinnerTop cookie policy.

What are cookies?

Like most applications, CricWinnerTop uses cookies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet, or similar) to recognize it, remember preferences, gather statistics, and target advertisements. Cookies can include text, numbers or dates but not personal data. They pose no risk to your device (e.g., they cannot carry a virus). The following data processors can access the stored data: Microsoft Ltd., Google Ltd., Match2One AB, and Facebook Ltd.
Usage of our app implies your consent for the use of cookies, including third party cookies. However, you can delete or block cookies at any time. Details about the cookies stored on your device through your use of our app can be found at the bottom of this page.

Deleting or blocking cookies may result in less relevant advertisements for you and increased frequency of ad appearances. You may also find that the app doesn't function optimally and there may be content you can't access.

Cookies are also used in compiling log files from the app’s users. Like all digital platforms, we generate log files which contain an overview of 1) the app screens visited, 2) when they were accessed, and 3) the geographical location based on IP address. We can see only the city of access, not your precise location. Further information about our personal data processing, including your IP address, can be found in our privacy policy.
These log files are used for monitoring traffic and as a security measure. For example, IP addresses help us to prevent hacker attacks.
Log files, including IP-addresses are stored for 5 years before they are deleted.
We use cookies to a variety of ends, like optimizing our marketing strategies, improving user experience, and enhancing the technical functionality of our app.
The data we gather through cookies is used to study user behavior on the app. The collected data helps us to optimize our marketing efforts, improve user experience, perfectly tailor our app and target online ads, and make the app more functional for its users.

The use of cookies enables ongoing adaptations to the app to better meet user needs as identified through their behavior. Alongside us, certain third parties also install cookies on your device during the use of the app. More details about the third parties can be found under "Third party plugins that leave cookies".

What cookies do we use?

A list of cookies that are stored on your device when you use our app is located at the bottom of this page. In the following, we will detail different types of cookies. For instance, the app periodically uses heat maps, which trace user activity throughout the app. This data and associated information will be stored. Heat maps are a tool utilized to understand user movement on the app, which aids in optimization efforts.

Cookies used for analysis

CricWinnerTop uses "Google Analytics" and "Matomo" to ascertain the geographical regions from which users access the app. This is gauged via the users’ IP-addresses. The geographic location data is primarily utilized to ensure that we don't serve users from areas where online betting is prohibited.
The app also compiles anonymous statistics about the number of visits, the average time spent on the app, and which screens were accessed. This data helps us to tailor online ads to our audience.

Targeted advertising

When using CricWinnerTop, cookies are stored on your device to enable targeted and relevant online advertising on platforms like Facebook and Instagram, and on other websites. You might also see our banner ads displayed on other websites.
Targeted marketing efforts cease no later than 180 days from your last app visit. You can also avoid targeted ads by deleting or blocking cookies.
Third party plugins on the app, such as embedded Youtube videos, may also leave a cookie on your device. These come from third party services on the app.

Examples of third-party cookies include: Youtube – May occur on the app due to embedded videos.
These features are included to provide users with additional features (such as videos) and other benefits.

How long do we store user information?

Information on users’ interaction with our app is stored up to 26 months in Google Analytics. If a user accesses the app again within 26 months, the storage period restarts from that moment—a practice known as 'data retention'.
The collected data does not contain any personal information of the users. They aim to provide our users the best possible experience on our app.
Google Analytics data is stored up to 26 months to enable the generation of annual site development statistics, to retain comparable historical data, and to measure the site’s traffic.
Information related to users who have interacted with or visited the app via Facebook Marketing will be stored in a Facebook Pixel for up to 180 days, abiding by Facebook’s guidelines. All data is anonymised by Facebook.
The use of the Facebook Pixel aims to target ads and marketing from the site via Facebook and to present users with ads that match their interests and needs.

How can I avoid cookies?

If you do not wish to store cookies on your device, you can delete them at any time. This process is simple and quick. A guide to deleting cookies can be found at the following link: https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en
To prevent your device from storing cookies while you are online, you can choose to block all cookies or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. You can find a guide on how to change your browser settings to block cookies in the given link.
