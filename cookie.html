Cookies Policy


CricWinnerTop is an innovative platform offering fantasy sports management where sports enthusiasts can leverage their knowledge of the game. Our application allows users to assemble their team from real players, compete against others, and earn points based on real player performances in upcoming matches without placing any bets. This page explains the cookie policy of the CricWinnerTop application.

What Are Cookies?

As with many applications, CricWinnerTop utilizes cookies. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, phone, tablet, etc.) to recognize it, store preferences, compile statistics, and deliver targeted advertising. These files are made up of text, numbers, or dates but do not contain personal information, hence they cannot compromise the safety of your device (such as spreading a virus). Access to this data is available to certain data processors, including Microsoft Ltd., Google Ltd., Match2One AB, and Facebook Ltd.
Your use of our application indicates your acceptance of the use of cookies, encompassing third-party cookies as well. Nevertheless, you have the option to delete or block cookies at any moment. Further details about the cookies used through our application are provided at the end of this page.

Be aware that deleting or blocking cookies may lead to suboptimal app performance, potentially repetitive advertisements, and inaccessible content.

We utilize cookies to compile log files of user interactions within the app. Like all digital services, these logs provide a summary of visited screens, usage timings, and geographical locations derived from IP addresses; however, we only ascertain the city of access. More details about our personal data processing, which includes IP addresses, can be accessed in our privacy policy.
These logs serve as a tool for traffic analysis and as a safeguard, such as helping to thwart hacker activities.
IP address logs and associated data are retained for 5 years before deletion.
The overall purpose of our cookie usage encompasses marketing strategy optimization, user experience enhancement, and app functionality improvement.
The information gleaned through cookies provides invaluable insights into user behavior, which guides the refining of our marketing efforts and user experience, ensures the app's personalized finesse, targets online ads accurately, and heightens the application's utility for our audience.

Cookie usage enables us to continually refine the app according to user behavior insights. During your use of our app, certain third-parties also place cookies on your device. You can find more information on these parties under "Third-party plugins leaving cookies."

What Cookies Do We Use?

A summary of the cookies stored on your device by our app can be located at the bottom of this page. We use an array of cookies for different reasons; for example, heat maps tracing user activity throughout the app are periodically utilized and this information is stored.
Heat maps are instrumental in comprehending user navigation and interaction on the app, which assists in our ongoing optimization process.

Cookies Used for Analysis

CricWinnerTop employs tools such as "Google Analytics" and "Matomo" to assess where our users are accessing the app from, using their IP addresses. The collected geographic data ensures we operate within regions where fantasy sports are allowed.
Additionally, we compile anonymized statistics on visit counts, duration of app usage, and accessed screens, which underpins our tailored advertising strategies.

Targeted Advertising

Throughout your experience with CricWinnerTop, your device stores cookies enabling targeted advertising across various platforms such as Facebook and Instagram, as well as other websites. These advertisements may also appear as banner ads on third-party sites.
Our targeted marketing activities are discontinued no later than 180 days after your last visit to the app. To avoid targeted advertisements, you can opt to delete or block cookies.
Third-party plugins incorporated into our app, for example, YouTube videos embedded within content, may also place cookies on your device.

How Long Do We Store User Information?

Data involving user interaction with our app is stored within Google Analytics for a maximum of 26 months. This retention period restarts each time a user revisits the app, a process known as 'data retention'.
The aggregated data is anonymous and focuses on enhancing the user experience on our app.
We retain Google Analytics data for 26 months to facilitate the development of annual statistics, to maintain historical comparability, and to analyze traffic patterns.
Interactions related to users who have visited the app through Facebook Marketing are recorded in a Facebook Pixel for a period up to 180 days, in compliance with Facebook's policies. All such data is anonymized by Facebook.
The rationale behind the Facebook Pixel is to customize site advertising on Facebook, ensuring users receive ads that are more aligned with their preferences and interests.

How Can I Avoid Cookies?

Should you prefer not to store cookies on your device, the deletion process is straightforward and can be done at any time. For guidance on cookie deletion, refer to this link: https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en
To prevent cookies from being stored while browsing, block all cookies in your browser settings or choose to be notified before a cookie is set. Instructions for modifying browser settings to block cookies can be found via the provided link.
