F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What range of fruits does FruitFiesta offer?

FruitFiesta offers a broad variety of fresh fruits, ranging from commonplace selections such as apples and bananas to exotic choices including dragon fruit and passionfruit. We take the utmost pride in the diversity and quality of the fruits we offer.

Do you ensure organic and pesticide-free products?

Absolutely, we acknowledge the importance of organic, pesticide-free products for the well-being of our customers. All our fruits are sourced from trusted farms that follow organic farming methods ensuring safe and healthy products.

Do you facilitate bulk orders?

Yes, FruitFiesta caters to bulk orders keeping in mind the requirements of businesses, events, or personal needs. Kindly contact us with the details, and we will be more than happy to serve you.

Do you offer home delivery services?

Absolutely, FruitFiesta provides home delivery services to make your purchase convenient and hassle-free. Feel free to place your order, and relish the freshness of our fruits at your doorstep.

How do you maintain the freshness of fruits?

FruitFiesta maintains high-quality preservation and storage systems, allowing us to keep the fruits in their freshest state from farm to table. Every fruit is handled with utmost care to ensure premium quality upon delivery.

Are seasonal fruits available?’

Yes, in addition to our year-round fruits, we offer a range of seasonal fruits. We believe the best taste and nutritional benefits are found during the fruit's natural harvesting period, and we strive to provide this farm-fresh goodness to our customers.

Can we visit your farm?

Yes, we heartily welcome customers to visit our farms. It’s a delightful experience where you can learn about our farming practices and see first-hand how we cultivate and harvest your fruits.

Cookie settings

Cookies are small pieces of data sent from a website and stored on the user's computer by the web browser while it is running. The browser stores each message in a small file called a cookie. When you request another page from the server, your browser sends the cookie back to the server. Cookies have been developed as a reliable mechanism that allows websites to remember information or record user activity when browsing pages.

  Necessary ∨

Strictly necessary cookies - usually these cookies are necessary for the operation of the site. They can help you show the user the information they need, customize their experience, and allow you to implement and maintain security features. As a rule, without these cookies, the operation of the site is not possible or its functioning may be severely impaired.

  Functional ∨

Functional cookies - usually these cookies are used to improve the functional characteristics of your site and make it easier for users to use. These cookies remember the settings selected by users (for example, language and time zone settings). With the help of these files, users can avoid changing the settings each time they visit the site.

  Statistics ∨

Performance cookies - usually these cookies show whether the user has visited your site before. Analytical cookies allow you to recognize and count the number of users of your site and see how these users move around your site.

  Marketing ∨

Marketing, targeting, and advertising cookies – Usually, these cookies record visitors' visits to your site, the pages they visited, and the links they clicked on. This information is used to make the advertisements displayed on it more relevant to the interests of the user. Advertising cookies allow you to know if the user has already seen a specific advertisement or an advertisement of a certain type.
