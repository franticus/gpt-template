Understanding Cookies

Cookies are tiny data pieces collected on your device when you browse our website. They enhance our site's functionality, customize your furniture shopping journey, and help us garner important user data.

Our Use of Cookies

We deploy cookies to:

  • Retain your choices to tailor your furniture discovery experience.
  • Accumulate anonymous data about user engagements on our platform.
  • Enable various interactive elements on our site to amplify your browsing journey.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we utilize are classified into session and persistent, detailed as follows:

  • Session cookies: These thrive only during your online session and are erased once you shut your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These stay on your device across multiple browsing instances for a specified time. They facilitate us in identifying you on your return, ensuring a seamless furniture shopping journey.

Your Cookie Choices

The option to accept or decline cookies is yours. Though most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can tweak your settings to deny them. Be informed that this might affect the website's prime performance and your furniture hunting experience.
