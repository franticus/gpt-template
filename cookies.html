Cookie Policy

Welcome to Noftebok Real Estate's Cookie Policy. This document outlines how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit.

Our website uses both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

Why We Use Cookies

1. Essential Cookies:

  • Ensure the proper functioning of our website.
  • Help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts.
  • Remember your choices, such as language preferences.

2. Analytical and Performance Cookies:

  • Collect information about how visitors use our site.
  • Help us improve the user experience by identifying and resolving issues.
  • Provide statistical data on website usage.

3. Advertising Cookies:

  • Deliver more relevant ads to you based on your interests.
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad.
  • Measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies and delete them. However, blocking essential cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

By using our site with your browser settings adjusted to accept cookies, you consent to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy.

Cookie Security Measures

Ensuring the security of your data is crucial. Our website employs encryption and secure protocols to safeguard the information stored in cookies. This protects your privacy and prevents unauthorized access to your personal details.

We regularly update our security measures to stay ahead of potential threats. Our commitment to your online safety is reflected in the continuous improvement of our security protocols.

Cookie Policy Updates

1. Regular Review:

We conduct periodic reviews of our Cookie Policy to ensure it aligns with the latest legal requirements and technological advancements. Any updates made will be reflected in the most recent version of this policy.

2. Notification of Changes:

If there are significant changes to our Cookie Policy, we will provide prominent notice on our website or notify you via email. We want to keep you informed about how we use cookies and any alterations to our practices.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include content from third-party providers, each of whom may use cookies. We do not control the use of these cookies, and you should refer to the privacy policies of these third parties for information on how they utilize cookies.

We strive to collaborate with trusted partners who share our commitment to user privacy. However, we cannot be responsible for the content and cookie practices of external websites.

Managing Cookie Preferences

1. Browser Settings:

You have the option to adjust your browser settings to manage cookie preferences. This allows you to control whether to accept or reject cookies and delete them when desired.

2. Cookie Consent Tools:

Utilize cookie consent tools available on our website to customize your preferences. These tools provide you with granular control over the types of cookies you are willing to accept.
