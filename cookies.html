Understanding Cookies

Cookies are tiny portions of data stored on your device when you visit our website. They play an essential role in enhancing our website's efficiency, customizing your online shopping experience, and helping us amass vital analytics.

Our Use of Cookies

We deploy cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to customize your shopping experience.
  • Collect anonymous data related to user activities on our website.
  • Enable various features on our site to uplift your gift and souvenir browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We employ both session and persistent cookies, classified as follows:

  • Session cookies: These cookies are operational only whilst your individual online session and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device over multiple sessions and a set duration, enabling us to recognize you during each visit, assuring an efficient shopping expedition.

Your Cookie Preferences

The decision to accept or decline cookies is up to you. Although most web browsers automatically consent to cookies, you can alter your settings to deny them. Please be aware, however, that this action may disrupt the site's optimal functioning and your shopping experience.
