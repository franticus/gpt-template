Cookie Policy

Effective from: 10-31-2023

About Cookies

Cookies are small data fragments stored on your device when you visit our website. They intensify our site's capacity, personalize your vintage shopping expedition, and assist us in gathering essential user data.

Usage of Cookies

We employ cookies to:

  • Retain your preferences to customize your vintage fashion exploration.
  • Collect anonymous data about user interactions on our platform.
  • Enable various interactive features on our site enhancing your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies Used

We utilize cookies grouped into session and persistent, detailed as follows:

  • Session cookies: These exist only during your active online session and disappear once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These remain on your device over multiple browsing sessions for a sspecified time. They help us recognize you on your return, ensuring a smooth vintage shopping excursion.

Your Cookie Preferences

The choice to accept or refuse cookies is yours. While most browsers automatically allow cookies, you can alter your settings to reject them. Be aware that this might influence the website's optimal performance and your vintage fashion finding experience.
