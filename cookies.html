Cookie Policy

Effective from: 10-31-2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are bits of data that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They enable functionality, customize your browsing experience with exquisite jewelry selections, and let us gather important insights into user preferences.

How We Use Cookies

We apply cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to tailor your jewelry search effortlessly.
  • Collect anonymous statistics on our customer's site engagements.
  • Empower a suite of interactive features to improve your shopping experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Our cookies fall into two main categories, temporary and permanent, as explained:

  • Temporary cookies: These exist during your session and disappear once you close your browser.
  • Permanent cookies: These cookies remain after you close your browser, helping us recognize your device when you return, thus ensuring a consistent and refined shopping experience.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

It is your choice to accept or deny the use of cookies. You can configure your browser to reject cookies, but please note that doing so may interfere with the seamless functioning of our website and your luxury shopping experience.
