Cookie Policy

Effective from: 10-31-2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device when you visit our site. They facilitate functionality, enhance your learning experience by retaining your preferences, and allow us to collect important insights into user behaviors.

How We Use Cookies

We utilize cookies to:

  • Remember your selections and preferences, making your guitar learning process quick and efficient.
  • Compile anonymous statistics about how our users interact with our site.
  • Enable a variety of interactive features to enrich your learning experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use are categorized into temporary and permanent to serve the following purposes:

  • Temporary cookies: These are active during your session and are deleted after you close your browser.
  • Permanent cookies: These remain on your device after you close your browser and allow us to recognize you upon return, offering a more personalized and consistent learning experience.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the option to either accept or decline the use of cookies. You can set up your browser to refuse cookies, but be aware that this may impact the functionality of our site and your learning experience.
