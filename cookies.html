What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny data files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are essential to the functioning of our website, optimizing your online shopping experience, and providing us valuable insights.

How We Use Cookies

Our site uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to customize your shopping experience.
  • Collect anonymized data on user interactions within our site.
  • Enable various interactive features on our website to enhance your fashion shopping experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses two categories of cookies, transient and persistent, as explained below:

  • Transient cookies: These cookies are present only during your online session and vanish after you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device across multiple sessions and for a predefined period. It helps to identify you during subsequent visits, ensuring a consistent shopping experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

Whether to allow or not allow cookies is your decision. While most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you can change your browser settings to decline them. However, this may disrupt the optimal functioning of the website and affect your shopping experience.
