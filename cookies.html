Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are saved on your device when you visit a website. They help enhance the performance of our website, improve your online shopping experience, and provide us with valuable insights.

Our Use of Cookies

We utilize cookies to:

  • Remember your personal preferences and customize your shopping experience accordingly.
  • Collect anonymous data on user behavior during their website use.
  • Enable various interactive features on our website to enrich your footwear browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We can categorize the cookies we use into temporary or persistent, as outlined below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies stay on your device only for the duration of your online session and are erased once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device for multiple online sessions over a specified time. They assist us in recognizing you when you return, ensuring a seamless shopping experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

You can opt to accept or decline cookies. While most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you can alter your settings to reject them. However, understand that this might impact the website's full performance and your shopping experience.
