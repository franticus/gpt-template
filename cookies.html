Effective from: 10-31-2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are compact pieces of data stored on your device when you visit a website. They are essential to enhance the functionality of our website, personalize your booking experience, and allow us to gather relevant data.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to personalize your booking experience.
  • Gather anonymous data about user engagement on our website.
  • Enable various interaction features on our website to enhance your browsing experience during journey planning.

Types of Cookies We Use

We classify the cookies we use into session and persistent cookies, explained as follows:

  • Session cookies: These cookies stay on your device only for the duration of your current session. They disappear once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device over several browser sessions and for a stipulated time. They enable us to recognize you on subsequent visits for a consistent browsing experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

You have the full right to accept or decline cookies. While most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you can adjust your settings to refuse them. Please note, this might affect the website's optimal performance and your booking experience.
