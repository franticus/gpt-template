Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are essential in optimizing the website's performance, enriching your online learning experience, and providing us, the website owners, with necessary analytics.

Our Cookie Usage

We use cookies to:

  • Personalize your learning experience by remembering your individual preferences.
  • Gather anonymous analytical data about user behaviour during their learning journey.
  • Enable various interactive features on our website, enhancing your language learning experience.

Our Cookie Categories

The cookies we use fall into the following categories: temporary and persistent, as outlined below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies remain on your device during your online session and are deleted when you close your web browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device across multiple online sessions for a specific duration. They help us recognize you when you return to our website, ensuring a consistent learning experience.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the option to accept or decline cookies. While most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can alter your settings to refuse them. However, be informed that this may affect the full functionality of the website and impact your learning experience.
