Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data packets that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are integral to improving the functioning of our website, optimizing your online shopping experience, and allowing us to collect valuable insights.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to customize your shopping experience.
  • Gather anonymized data about user interactions on our site.
  • Facilitate various interactive features on our website to enhance your fresh fruits browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies we use into transient and persistent ones, as elaborated below:

  • Transient cookies: These cookies remain on your device only during your online session and vanish once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device over several browser sessions and for a fixed duration. They enable us to recognize you each time you return, ensuring a consistent shopping experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

The decision to accept or decline cookies is entirely up to you. Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you can adjust your settings to decline them. Please note that this might affect the website's optimal functioning and your shopping experience.
