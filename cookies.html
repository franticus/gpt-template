Cookie Policy

Welcome to Ligyting Language School's Cookie Policy. This document outlines how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our online language learning platform. By continuing to browse and use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function more efficiently and provide information to website owners. Cookies help enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and activities.

Our platform uses both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device until manually deleted) to offer a personalized and seamless learning experience.

How We Use Cookies

Ligyting uses cookies for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Remembering your login details for a hassle-free sign-in process.
  • Analyzing website traffic to improve our services and user experience.
  • Customizing content and language preferences based on your interactions.
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our platform.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the option to manage and control cookies on your device. Most web browsers allow you to configure settings to accept, reject, or delete cookies. However, please note that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of certain features on our platform.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at info@madrahon.com.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update and modify our Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology and legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, so please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

At Ligyting Language School, we utilize different types of cookies to enhance your learning experience. These include essential cookies that are necessary for the basic functionality of our platform, performance cookies to analyze user behavior, and functionality cookies to remember your preferences.

Additionally, third-party cookies may be used to integrate external services and features, such as social media plugins. These cookies are subject to the respective third parties' privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them for more information.

Cookie Consent

When you visit our website, a cookie banner will appear, seeking your consent to use cookies. By clicking "Accept" or continuing to browse, you agree to the placement of cookies on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or the provided options in our cookie banner.

Data Security

Your privacy and data security are paramount to us. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information collected through cookies. Our secure protocols and encryption technologies ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Rest assured that the information collected is used solely for improving our services and providing you with a tailored learning experience. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Opting Out

If you prefer not to allow cookies, you can adjust your browser settings to block or delete them. Keep in mind that opting out of certain cookies may impact the functionality of our platform, and you may need to manually adjust some preferences each time you visit.

Children's Privacy

Ligyting Language School is committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our services are intended for users aged 13 and above. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately to address the issue.

Questions and Feedback
