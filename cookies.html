Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are instrumental in enhancing the website's performance, enriching your browsing experience, and providing us, the website owners, with essential analytics.

Our Cookie Usage

We use cookies to:

  • Personalize your rental experience by memorizing your individual preferences.
  • Gather anonymous analytical data about user behaviour during their rental journey.
  • Enable various interactive features on our website, enhancing your rental experience.

Our Cookie Categories

The cookies we use can be categorized as follows: temporary and persistent, as outlined below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies remain on your device for the duration of your online session and are removed when you close your web browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for multiple online sessions over a set period. They help us to recognize you when you return to our website, ensuring a consistent rental experience.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the freedom to accept or decline cookies. Despite most browsers automatically accepting cookies, you can adjust your settings to refuse them. Note, however, that this might affect the full functionality of the website, impacting your rental experience.
