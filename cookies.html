Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are saved on your device when you visit a website. They assist in enhancing the performance of our website, improving your online shopping experience, and allow us to gain useful insights.

Our Use of Cookies

We utilize cookies to:

  • Recognize your personal preferences and modify your shopping experience according to them.
  • Gather anonymous data regarding user interaction with our website.
  • Facilitate various interactive features on our website to enhance your pet product browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies we use into temporary or persistent, as clarified below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies exist on your device solely for the period of your online session and are detached once you conclude your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies persist on your device over multiple online sessions and for a predefined time. They enable us in recognizing you on returning, guaranteeing a consistent shopping experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

You have options to either accept or decline cookies. While most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies, you can adjust your settings to deny them. However, be aware that this might affect the full performance of the website and your shopping experience.
