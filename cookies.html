Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data saved on your device when you visit a website. They are important for improving our website's performance, personalizing your online learning experience, and aiding us in collecting useful observations.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your choices to personalize your learning journey.
  • Gather anonymized data concerning user engagements on our website.
  • Activate assorted interactive features on our site to better your course browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we utilize can be divided into session and persistent cookies, as follows:

  • Session cookies: These cookies are active only during your individual online session and are removed when you quit your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies persist on your device through multiple sessions and a specified period, enabling us to identify you for each visit, assuring a proficient learning process.

Your Cookie Preferences

The power to accept or reject cookies rests with you. While most internet browsers automatically accept cookies, you can modify your settings to reject them. However, please keep in mind that this action may affect the website's optimal operation and your learning experience.
