Cookie Policy

Effective from: October 31, 2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device when you visit our site. They facilitate functionality, enhance your shoe shopping experience by retaining your preferences, and allow us to collect important insights into user behaviors.

At Barekube, we recognize the significance of your privacy and strive to make your online experience as enjoyable and personalized as possible. This Cookie Policy outlines how we use cookies to achieve these goals.

How We Use Cookies

We utilize cookies to:

  • Remember your selections and preferences, making your shoe shopping process quick and efficient.
  • Compile anonymous statistics about how our users interact with our site.
  • Enable a variety of interactive features to enrich your shopping experience.

These cookies play a crucial role in providing you with a seamless and tailored browsing experience tailored to your unique needs and preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use are categorized into temporary and permanent to serve the following purposes:

  • Temporary cookies: These are active during your session and are deleted after you close your browser.
  • Permanent cookies: These remain on your device after you close your browser and allow us to recognize you upon return, offering a more personalized and consistent shopping experience.

Each type of cookie contributes to the overall functionality and performance of our website, ensuring a positive and efficient interaction with our shoe shopping platform.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the option to either accept or decline the use of cookies. You can set up your browser to refuse cookies, but be aware that this may impact the functionality of our site and your shopping experience.

We respect your autonomy in choosing your cookie preferences and encourage you to tailor your settings based on your comfort and browsing needs.

Privacy and Security

Ensuring the privacy and security of your information is paramount at Barekube. Our advanced security measures, including encryption and data protection protocols, safeguard your sensitive data.

We continually update our security practices to stay ahead of emerging threats, providing you with a secure environment for exploring our shoe collection and shopping options.
