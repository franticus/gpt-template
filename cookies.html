Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data packets that reside on your device upon accessing a website. They play a pivotal role in enhancing our website's operation, fine-tuning your online hiring experience, and enabling us to gather precious insights.

Our Use of Cookies

We leverage cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to tailor your hiring experience.
  • Accumulate anonymized data concerning user interactions on our site.
  • Activate diverse interactive capacities on our website to refine your design services browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We classify the cookies we employ into transient and persistent, as elucidated below:

  • Transient cookies: These cookies linger on your device solely throughout your online session and disappear once you shut your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies persist on your device over multiple browser sessions and for a specified duration. They empower us to identify you upon each return, assuring a stable hiring experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

The choice to accept or refuse cookies is yours. While most web browsers instinctively consent to cookies, you can modify your settings to reject them. However, do note that this can hinder the website's peak operation and your hiring experience.
