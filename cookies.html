Cookie Policy

Effective from: 10-31-2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data pieces stored on your device when you visit our website. They improve our site's functionality, personalize your property search experience, and allow us to collect valuable user data.

Our Use of Cookies

We utilize cookies to:

  • Store your preferences to personalize your property search.
  • Gather anonymous data about user interactions on our platform.
  • Activate various interactive features on our site to enhance your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use are categorized into transitory and persistent, clarified below:

  • Transitory cookies: These are active on your device only during your online session and are removed once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These remain on your device across multiple browsing sessions for a specified period. They enable us to recognise you on your return, ensuring a smooth property search process.

Your Cookie Choices

The decision to accept or decline cookies belongs to you. While most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can modify your settings to refuse them. Please be aware that this might affect the website's optimal functioning and your property finding experience.
