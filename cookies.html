Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data packets that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are essential in enhancing our website's functionality, personalizing your online shopping experience, and enabling us to gather valuable insights.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences to customize your shopping experience.
  • Collect anonymized data regarding user interactions on our site.
  • Enable various interactive capabilities on our website to enhance your product browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize the cookies we use into transitory and persistent, as outlined below:

  • Transitory cookies: These cookies remain on your device only during your online session and are deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device across multiple browser sessions and for a specified period. They enable us to recognize you on every visit, ensuring a seamless shopping experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

The choice to accept or decline cookies is in your hands. Although most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you can amend your settings to decline them. However, please note that doing this may interfere with the website's optimal functionality and your shopping experience.
