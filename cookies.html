Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are essential in enhancing the website's performance, improving your online browsing experience, and providing us with valuable analytics.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your individual preferences and personalize your browsing experience accordingly.
  • Gather anonymous data on user behaviour during their website navigation.
  • Enable various interactive features on our platform to enhance your office space browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use can be classed as either temporary or persistent, as described below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies stay on your device only during your online session and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device across multiple online sessions for a specified period. They help us recognize you when you return, ensuring a consistent browsing experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

You have the option to either accept or reject cookies. While most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can modify your settings to refuse them. However, note that this may affect the website's full functionality and your browsing experience.
