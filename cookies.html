Cookie Policy

Effective from: 10-31-2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They play a crucial role in improving the website's performance, enhancing your browsing experience, and providing us, the website owners, with valuable analytics.

Our Cookie Usage

We use cookies to:

  • Customize your fitness experience by remembering your unique preferences.
  • Collect anonymous analytical data about user behavior.
  • Facilitate various interactive functions in our gym.

Our Cookie Categories

The cookies we use can be categorized as follows: temporary and persistent, as explained below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies remain on your device for the duration of your online session and are deleted when you close your web browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device over multiple online sessions for a defined period. They help us recognize you when you return to our gym.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the option to accept or decline cookies. While most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can adjust your settings to refuse them. Please note that this may impact the full functionality of the website, affecting your fitness experience.
