Cookie Policy

Welcome to Madrahon, where your botanical journey is our priority. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. By using Madrahon, you consent to our use of cookies as described below.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain data related to your visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a customized user experience, and inform website owners about visitors' activities.

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They are essential for the proper functioning of certain features.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period. They help us recognize you when you return to our site and analyze your interactions.
  • Third-party Cookies: Madrahon may use third-party services that install cookies on your device. These cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of the third parties.

Why We Use Cookies

Madrahon uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, personalize content, and understand how you interact with our website. The information collected helps us tailor our services to your preferences and optimize the performance of our site.

Cookies also enable us to gather statistical data about our website usage, such as the pages visited, time spent on each page, and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Your Cookie Choices
