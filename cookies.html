Cookie Policy

Effective from: 10-31-2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They aid in enhancing the performance of our website, improving your online booking experience, and allowing us to gain valuable insights.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Recall your personal preferences and adapt your booking experience accordingly.
  • Gather anonymous data regarding user interaction with our website.
  • Enable various interactive features on our website to enhance your photo studio browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We group the cookies we use into temporary or persistent, as explained below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies exist on your device only for the duration of your online session and are removed once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device over multiple online sessions and for a set duration. They allow us to recognize you when you return, ensuring a consistent booking experience.

Your Cookie Preferences

You have the option to accept or decline cookies. While most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies, you can adjust your settings to refuse them. However, be aware that this might affect the full performance of the website and your booking experience.
