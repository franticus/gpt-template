Cookie Policy

Effective from: November 29, 2023

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device when you visit our site. They aid functionality, enhance your learning experience by retaining preferences, and allow us to collect valuable insights into user interactions.

At Catyrine, we value your privacy and aim to make your online learning experience enjoyable and personalized. This Cookie Policy outlines how we use cookies to achieve these objectives.

How We Use Cookies

We utilize cookies to:

  • Remember your settings and preferences, streamlining the learning process.
  • Gather anonymous statistics about user interactions with our learning platform.
  • Enable various interactive features to enrich your learning journey.

These cookies play a crucial role in providing you with a seamless and customized learning experience tailored to your unique requirements.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use are classified as temporary and permanent to serve the following purposes:

  • Temporary cookies: These are active during your session and are deleted after you close your browser.
  • Permanent cookies: These persist on your device even after you close your browser, allowing us to recognize you upon return and offer a more personalized learning experience.

Each type of cookie contributes to the overall functionality and performance of our website, ensuring a positive and efficient interaction with our 3D graphics learning platform.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the option to accept or decline the use of cookies. You can set up your browser to reject cookies, but note that this may impact the functionality of our site and your learning experience.

We respect your autonomy in choosing your cookie preferences and encourage you to adjust your settings based on your comfort and learning needs.

Privacy and Security

Safeguarding the privacy and security of your information is paramount at Catyrine. Our advanced security measures, including encryption and data protection protocols, ensure the safety of your sensitive data.

We consistently update our security practices to stay ahead of emerging threats, providing you with a secure environment for exploring the realm of 3D graphics and learning opportunities.
