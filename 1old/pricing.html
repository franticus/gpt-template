Frequently Asked Questions

At Dettaglix, we strive to ensure an easy and rewarding collaboration for our valued clients. Should any questions arise, we’ve answered several common inquiries below.

What kind of interior design styles do you specialize in?

Dettaglix caters to a wide range of design styles to suit individual client needs. These include modern, minimalist, industrial, mid-century modern, Scandinavian, and more.

Are design consultations included in your service fees?

Yes, initial design consultations are included in our service fees. We believe in understanding your design aesthetic and functional requirements before embarking on the design process.

How do I begin my interior design journey with Dettaglix?

Starting your design journey with Dettaglix is simple. You can reach us through our website or via a phone call. Once in touch, we arrange consultation meetings to discuss your project further.

What is your policy on alterations and cancellations?

We have a fair policy permitting design alterations provided they've been discussed in due time. Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the scheduled service, depending on your service plan. For detailed information, please refer to our Terms & Conditions.

Do you provide services for commercial projects and big teams?

Indeed! Dettaglix is well-versed in managing commercial projects and large teams, understanding that commercial spaces represent the brand's identity. Please contact us for specific requirements.

Cookie settings

Cookies are small pieces of data sent from a website and stored on the user's computer by the web browser while it is running. The browser stores each message in a small file called a cookie. When you request another page from the server, your browser sends the cookie back to the server. Cookies have been developed as a reliable mechanism that allows websites to remember information or record user activity when browsing pages.

  Necessary ∨

Strictly necessary cookies - usually these cookies are necessary for the operation of the site. They can help you show the user the information they need, customize their experience, and allow you to implement and maintain security features-skdfjb. As a rule, without these cookies, the operation of the site is not possible or its functioning may be severely impaired.

  Functional ∨

Functional cookies - usually these cookies are used to improve the functional characteristics of your site and make it easier for users to use. These cookies remember the settings selected by users (for example, language and time zone settings). With the help of these files, users can avoid changing the settings each time they visit the site.

  Statistics ∨

Performance cookies - usually these cookies show whether the user has visited your site before. Analytical cookies allow you to recognize and count the number of users of your site and see how these users move around your site.

  Marketing ∨

Marketing, targeting, and advertising cookies – Usually, these cookies record visitors' visits to your site, the pages they visited, and the links they clicked on. This information is used to make the advertisements displayed on it more relevant to the interests of the user. Advertising cookies allow you to know if the user has already seen a specific advertisement or an advertisement of a certain type.
