Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data microfiles stored on your digital device when you browse a website. The primary purposes served by cookies include enhancing the functionality of the website and enriching the user's browsing experience, along with delivering integral analytics data to the website operators.

Our Utilization of Cookies

Our utilization of cookies encompasses:

  • Delivering a custom browsing experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Compilation of anonymized analytical data on user behaviors.
  • Facilitating certain social media features.

Cookie Categories Used

We employ both session and persistent cookies, as described below:

  • Session cookies: These are fleeting cookies retained on your device during your browsing session. Upon closing your web browser, they are erased automatically.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device across numerous browsing sessions for a predetermined duration. They help us identify you on your return visits.

Your Cookie Options

You also have the prerogative to either accept or decline cookies. While the majority of browsers accept cookies by default, you can alter settings to block cookies. However, please note that doing so might affect the functionality of the website and degrade the user's experience.
