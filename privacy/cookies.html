Understanding Cookies

Cookies encompass tiny informational bits stored as files in your personal or mobile device when you browse a webpage. They serve dual purposes - enhancing the functionality of the website while optimizing the browsing experience for the user, and providing crucial website activity insights to the site operators.

Our Implementation of Cookies

Our usage of cookies is characterized by:

  • Facilitating a personalized browsing experience by remembering your preferred settings.
  • Accumulating anonymous data regarding user activity for analysis.
  • Activating specific social media functionalities.

Varieties of Cookies Implemented

We employ both ephemeral and standing cookies in the manners specified below:

  • Ephemeral cookies: These are provisional cookies stored on your device while you interact with our site. They are automatically purged once you terminate your browser session.
  • Standing cookies: These files persist in your device beyond a single browser session for a specified duration. They enable us to identify you as a returnee.

User Cookie Preferences

Users reserve the authority to accept or deny cookies. While the default settings of most browsers accept cookies, you maintain the liberty to modify the setting to reject cookies. However, please consider that this could potentially have repercussions on site functionality and user experience.
