Understanding Cookies

Cookies are minuscule data files stored on your computer or handheld device when you access a website. These are largely incorporated to ensure greater efficiency of websites and to enrich user experience, as well as to gather site-usage information for the site administrators.

Our Utilization of Cookies

Our reasons for using cookies include the following:

  • To enhance your website exploration by recalling your preferences.
  • To compile anonymous user behavior statistics for analytics purposes.
  • To enable certain social media capabilities.

Categories of Cookies Employed

We utilize both session and persistent cookies, as outlined below:

  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies are saved in your device's memory while you navigate through the site. Once you exit the browser, these are automatically erased.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies are retained on your device for a predetermined duration, persisting even beyond closing your browser. They aid in identifying you as a repeat visitor.

Personal Cookie Preferences

You have the choice to accept or decline cookies. While the default setting for most browsers is to automatically accept cookies, you can alter this to decline cookies according to your preference. Please note that doing so may limit the site's overall functionality and your user experience.
