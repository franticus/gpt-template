Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored on your electronic device when you visit a website. They are essential for improving the efficiency of a website, enhancing your browsing experience, and providing us, the owners of the website, with valuable analytical data.

Our Cookie Usage

We use cookies to:

  • Customize your child's online learning experience by remembering their unique preferences.
  • Collect anonymous analytical data about user behavior.
  • Facilitate various interactive learning functions.

Our Cookie Categories

The cookies we use fall into two categories: temporary and persistent, as described below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies remain on your child's device for the duration of their online session and disappear when they close the web browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your child's device over multiple online sessions for a defined period. They help us identify your child when they return to our website.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the option to accept or decline cookies. While most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can adjust these settings to refuse them. Please be aware that this may impact the full functionality of the website, and your child's learning experience may be affected.
