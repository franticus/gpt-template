Cookies Explained

Cookies embody tiny data nuggets stored on your electronic device while you use a website. They play a vital role in augmenting the website's functionality and enriching your browsing experience, and also provide crucial analytical data to us, the website administrators.

How We Use Cookies

Our implementation of cookies revolves around:

  • Tailoring your browsing experience by remembering your individual preferences.
  • Gathering anonymized analytical data on user behavior.
  • Enabling certain social media functionalities.

Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize both short-term and long-term cookies, as delineated below:

  • Short-term cookies: These are temporary cookies preserved on your device during your browsing session. They vanish when you close your internet browser.
  • Long-term cookies: These cookies stay on your device through multiple browsing sessions for a specified period. They assist us in recognizing you when you return to our website.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You hold the power to accept or reject cookies. Most browsers are set to accept cookies by default, but you can modify these settings to refuse cookies. Bear in mind, though, that this could impair the full functionality of the website and downgrade your user experience.
