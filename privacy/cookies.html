What are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny data files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are mainly designed to optimize the performance of websites and enhance user experience, as well as to collect information about site usage for website administrators.

How We Use Cookies

Our usage of cookies entails the following purposes:

  • To improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferred settings.
  • To gather anonymous data about user behavior for analytical purposes.
  • To facilitate specific social media features.

Types of Cookies We Use

We utilize both session and persistent cookies, as described below:

  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies are stored in your device's memory while you browse the site. They are automatically deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device for a set period, remaining even after you shut your browser. They help to recognize you as a return user.

Your Cookie Preferences

You have the liberty to either accept or reject cookies. While most browsers are set to automatically accept cookies by default, you can adjust this setting to deny cookies based on your preference. Please note that doing so may impact the site's overall performance and your user experience.
