Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data that are saved on your electronic device during a website visit. They are crucial to improving the efficiency of a website, enhance your browsing experience, and provide us, the proprietors of the website, with insightful analytical data.

Our Cookie Utilization

We utilise cookies to:

  • Customize your browsing journey by recalling your unique preferences.
  • Gather anonymous analytical data about user behaviour.
  • Facilitate various social media functions.

Our Cookie Categories

The cookies we use fall into transient and enduring categories, specified below:

  • Transient cookies: These cookies remain on your device for the span of your online session. They vanish after you shut the web browser.
  • Enduring cookies: These cookies stay on your device over several online sessions for a defined duration. They assist us in identifying you when you return to our website.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to accept or decline cookies. Although most browsers automatically accept cookies, you can alter these settings to refuse cookies. Please be aware that this could affect the complete functionality of the website and your user experience might suffer.
