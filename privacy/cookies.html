What Are Cookies?

Cookies constitute small pieces of information that are stored on your digital device when visiting a website. They are essential to improve the site's performance, enhance your surfing experience, and allow us, the website owners, to access valuable analytical information.

Our Use of Cookies

We deploy cookies to:

  • Personalize your navigation experience by remembering your specific preferences.
  • Collect unidentified analytical data regarding user interactions.
  • Enable various social media features.

Types of Cookies We Deploy

Our cookies are categorized into temporary and persistent types, described below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies are retained on your device for the duration of your web session. They disappear once you close the web browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device over multiple web sessions for a certain time. They help us recognize you when you revisit our website.

Options Pertaining to Cookies

It's your prerogative to either accept or decline cookies. Most browsers default to accepting cookies, however, you have the option to change this setting to reject cookies. Please note that this may compromise the full optimization of the website and your user experience could be affected.
