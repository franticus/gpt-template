What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text-based files that are saved in your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Their primary function is to enhance website operation and improve the user's experience, in addition to providing useful insights about site activity to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

The ways in which we utilize cookies include:

  • Enhancing your browsing journey by recalling your preferred settings.
  • Collecting anonymous user behavior data for analysis.
  • Enabling certain social media functionalities.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session and persistent cookies in the following ways:

  • Session cookies: These are temporary cookies held in your device's memory while you navigate the site. They are automatically cleared when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a predetermined duration, persisting even after closing the browser. They help us recognize you as a repeat visitor.

Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to either accept or decline cookies. Though most web browsers are configured to automatically accept cookies, you can modify this setting to reject cookies according to your preference. Be aware, however, this might affect the site's performance and your user experience.
