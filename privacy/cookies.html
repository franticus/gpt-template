Deciphering Cookies

Cookies are minute data fragments saved as files on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They fulfill two major roles - improving the website's functionality and the browsing experience for the user, while offering essential site activity data to the website administrators.

Our Application of Cookies

Our approach to cookies involves:

  • Providing a tailored browsing experience by remembering your favorite settings.
  • Gathering anonymized data pertaining to user activity for analysis.
  • Enabling certain social media functionalities.

Types of Cookies Used

We utilize both temporary and persistent cookies, in the ways outlined below:

  • Temporary cookies: These are transitory cookies stored on your device while you engage with our site. They are automatically erased when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These files linger on your device beyond a single browser session for a specified period. They allow us to recognize you on subsequent visits.

User Cookie Choices

Users have the capability to allow or refuse cookies. Although most browsers are set by default to accept cookies, you have the option to adjust the setting to decline cookies. Nonetheless, it's important to understand that this might impact site functionality and the user experience.
