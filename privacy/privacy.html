4. Cookie Policy

VeggieDelight employs "Cookies", along with other technologies and third-party services, to personalize user experiences, deliver pertinent advertising content, and remember client preferences.

A cookie is a chunk of data transferred from the site to the visitor's device, and then sent back by the visitor's device each time they return to the site. Cookies allow us to better our understanding of user behavior, track user engagement, and remember site preferences. Visitors who have issues with cookies should modify their browser settings prior to accessing VeggieDelight's website. Please note that turning off cookies may affect the site's functionality.

Choosing to continue to use our site without altering your cookie settings means you agree to VeggieDelight's use of cookies.
