4. Cookies Usage

Gastronomad makes use of "Cookies", coupled with efficient technologies and third-party services, to customize your online experience, offer pertinent advertisement content, and remember your preferences for your gear.

A cookie is a set of data stored on a visitor's device by the website and dispatched by the visitor's browser each time they revisit the site. Cookies upgrade our understanding of our users, track their site engagement, and learn their website preferences. Visitors not at ease with having cookies on their devices should adjust their browser configurations prior to accessing Gastronomad's website. Notably, disabling cookies might affect our website's functioning.

By choosing to continue the use of our website without adjusting your cookie settings, you are signaling your acceptance of Gastronomad's use of cookies.
