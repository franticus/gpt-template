4. Use of Cookies

Lens Loft leverages "Cookies", efficient technologies, and services provided by third parties to personalize your online navigating experience, to display relevant advertorial content, and to save your preferences on your computing device.

A cookie is a data item that is saved on a visitor’s device by a website and provided by the visitor’s web browser each time they visit the site. Cookies help us identify our visitors, track their usage of our website, and understand their site preference. Visitors who do not wish to accept cookies should modify their browser settings prior to using Lens Loft's site. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the site’s functional performance.

By continuing to navigate our site without altering your cookie settings, you are consenting to Lens Loft's use of cookies.
