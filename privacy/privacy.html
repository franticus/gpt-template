4. Use of Cookies

Mystic Muse uses "Cookies", competent technologies, and services provided by others to personalize your online experience, to show appropriate advertising and to store your preferences in your computer system.

A cookie is a chunk of data that is stored in a visitor’s computer system by a website and is provided by the visitor’s browser each time they visit the website. Cookies help us recognize our visitors, track their usage pattern with our site, and understand their website preference settings. Those visitors who prefer not to accept cookies need to configure their browsers accordingly before using Mystic Muse’s websites. Please be informed that disabling cookies may hinder the site’s proper functioning.

You, by continuing to use our website without altering your cookie settings, acknowledge and sanction Mystic Muse's use of cookies.
