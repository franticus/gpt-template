4. Our Use of Cookies

TastyTacos utilizes "Cookies", alongside competent technologies and third-party services, to personalize your online browsing, display applicable advertising content, and remember your preferences for your device.

A cookie is a packet of data stored on a visitor's device by a website and provided by the visitor's web browser each time they revisit the website. Cookies enhance our understanding of our visitors, track their website usage, and comprehend their website preference. Visitors who do not wish to have cookies on their computers should adjust their browser settings before using TastyTacos' website. Please be aware that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of the site.

By continuing to use our website without changing your cookie settings, you are agreeing to the use of cookies by TastyTacos.
