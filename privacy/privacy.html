4. Cookie Policy

ImmoNex uses "Cookies," along with other technologies and third-party services, to personalize real estate experiences, deliver relevant property listings, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is a piece of data sent from the site to the visitor's device, which is then sent back by the visitor's device each time they return to the site. Cookies help us better understand client behavior, track property interests, and remember site preferences. Users who have concerns about cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing ImmoNex's real estate services. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.

Continuing to use our real estate services without changing your cookie settings signifies your agreement to ImmoNex's use of cookies.
