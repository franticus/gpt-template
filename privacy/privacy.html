4. Cookie Policy

NatureNook uses "Cookies," along with other technologies and third-party services, to personalize user experiences, deliver relevant content, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is a piece of data sent from the site to the visitor's device, which is then sent back by the visitor's device each time they return to the site. Cookies help us better understand user behavior, track user engagement, and remember site preferences. Visitors who have concerns about cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing NatureNook's website. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.

Continuing to use our site without changing your cookie settings signifies your agreement to NatureNook's use of cookies.
