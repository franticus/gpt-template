4. Implementation of Cookies

ActiveLuxe utilizes "Cookies", integrated with proficient technologies and third-party services, to personalize your online experience, display relevant advertising content, and remember your preferences for your device.

A cookie is a set of data placed on a visitor's device by the website and delivered by the visitor's browser each time they return to the site. Cookies boost our understanding of our visitors, track their site usage, and learn their website preferences. Visitors uncomfortable with having cookies on their machines should configure their browser settings before accessing ActiveLuxe' website. Please note that disabling cookies might affect our site's functionality.

By persisting to use our site without modifying your cookie settings, you are consenting to the use of cookies by ActiveLuxe.
