4. Using Cookies

FruitFiesta uses "Cookies", along with other technologies and third-party services, to tailor user experience, deliver relevant advertising content, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is data attached to a user's device by the website and is dispatched by the user's browser each time the site is revisited. Cookies enhance our understanding of users, track engagement, and capture site preferences. Visitors uncomfortable with cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing FruitFiesta's website. Note that disabling cookies may affect the website's operation.

If you opt to continue using our website without modifying your cookie settings, you are agreeing to FruitFiesta's cookie usage.
