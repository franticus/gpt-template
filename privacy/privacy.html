4. Cookie Policy

ChildU uses "Cookies," along with other technologies and third-party services, to personalize learning experiences, deliver relevant content, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is a piece of data sent from the site to the visitor's device, which is then sent back by the visitor's device each time they return to the site. Cookies help us better understand student and parent behavior, track engagement, and remember site preferences. Users who have concerns about cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing ChildU's online school. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.

Continuing to use our online school without changing your cookie settings signifies your agreement to ChildU's use of cookies.
