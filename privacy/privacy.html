4. Cookie Policy

BuildWise uses "Cookies", along with other technologies and third-party services, to customize user experiences, provide relevant advertising content, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is a piece of data sent from the website to the visitor's device and is sent back by the visitor's browser each time they visit the site again. Cookies improve our understanding of user behaviour, monitor user engagement, and remember site preferences. Visitors who are uncomfortable with cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing the BuildWise's website. Note that disabling cookies may alter the site's functionality.

If you choose to continue using our website without adjusting your cookie settings, you consent to the use of cookies by BuildWise.
