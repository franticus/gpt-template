Navigating Our Learning Terms

Introduction

Certain terms in this document are defined for the sake of clarity, irrespective of their singular or plural usage.

Key Definitions

To enhance your understanding of our terms, the following definitions are set in our esteemed educational context:

  • Catyrine denotes our digital platform presenting a diverse array of 3D graphics courses available for exploration.
  • Graphics Courses signify the collection of learning options Catyrine presents for exploration.
  • Artistic Learner (also acknowledged as "Student" within this document) refers to any individual or entity who explores 3D graphics education through Catyrine.
  • Services include all student interactions, from course browsing to enrollment, support, and feedback.
