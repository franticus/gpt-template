Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They are crucial in improving the site's performance, enhancing your browsing experience, and providing insightful analytics.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and personalize your browsing experience accordingly.
  • Gather anonymous data on user interactions during website navigation.
  • Enable interactive features on our platform, improving your office space selection experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

The cookies we use can be classified as either session or persistent, detailed below:

  • Session cookies: These cookies remain on your device for the duration of your browsing session and are deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies stay on your device across multiple browsing sessions for a specific duration. They enable us to recognize you every time you return, ensuring a consistent browsing experience.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. While most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, you can alter your settings to block them. Please be aware that this may interfere with the website's full functionality and your experience while browsing our office spaces.
