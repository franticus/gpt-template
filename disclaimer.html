Understanding Cookies

Cookies are tiny data files retained on your device as you visit a website. They play a critical role in improving the website's performance, enhancing your online shopping adventure, and supplying us with useful analytics.

Our Cookie Usage

We employ cookies to:

  • Keep track of your individual preferences and personalise your shopping experience accordingly.
  • Collect anonymous data on user behaviour during their website navigation.
  • Enable various interactive features on our platform, enhancing your cosmetics shopping experience.

Our Cookie Categories

The cookies we utilise can be described as either temporary or persistent, as detailed below:

  • Temporary cookies: These cookies stay on your device only for the duration of your online session and disappear when you shut down your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device over multiple online sessions for a specific timeframe. They help us recognise you upon returning, guaranteeing a consistent shopping experience.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the choice to either accept or decline cookies. While most browsers by default accept cookies, you can adjust your settings to refuse them. However, please note that this might impact the full functionality of the website and your shopping experience.
