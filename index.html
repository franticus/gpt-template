Frequently Asked Questions

What types of support can I expect with my subscription?

Depending on your subscription level, SoftScribe offers varying degrees of support. At the Basic level, we provide standard support via email. Pro users enjoy priority support, while our Enterprise users have access to a dedicated account manager for personalized assistance.

Do you offer discounted pricing for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes, SoftScribe is proud to support nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact us for more information about our specific pricing structures for these entities.

Are there any user limits for each subscription package?

Our Pro plan is designed for teams up to 50 users. If you require use for a larger team, our Enterprise plan supports an unlimited number of users.

How difficult is it to integrate SoftScribe's software with my existing tech stack?

At SoftScribe, we strive to make our software as user-friendly as possible. Our APIs are designed to streamline integration with a variety of tech stacks. Furthermore, our dedicated support team is ready to assist with your integration needs.

What methods of payment do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, and American Express), PayPal, and bank transfer. If you have a specific payment requirement or query, please contact us.
