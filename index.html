Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you may have some questions. Here, we have compiled the most frequently asked queries to help you better understand our services.

  • What platforms do you use for website development?

    Our main development platforms are WordPress, Drupal, and Joomla for CMS, and Node.js, React.js, and Angular.js for custom web applications. However, we are open to using any platform according to the project requirements.

  • Do you provide post-launch website maintenance?

    Yes, we do provide post-launch website maintenance. We offer different maintenance and support plans suitable for your needs. Our maintenance plan includes regular updates, security audits, website optimization etc.

  • Will my website be mobile-friendly?

    Absolutely! All the websites we design are responsive which means they will be perfectly functional on all devices such as desktops, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones without losing any features.

  • How long does it take to develop a website?

    The development time of a website depends on various factors, including the complexity of the design, number of pages, custom functionality requirements, etc. However, a basic website usually takes around 1-2 weeks to complete.

  • Do you provide SEO services?

    Yes, we provide SEO services. As part of our website development process, we ensure that the website is optimized for search engines. However, for more advanced SEO needs, we have separate SEO packages.
