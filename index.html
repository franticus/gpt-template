F.A.Q

Need help? Have questions? We've got you covered. Choose DelishDrops for premier food delivery right to your doorstep.

  • What types of cuisine does DelishDrops offer?

    DelishDrops offers a wide variety of diverse and delicious cuisines; from Authentic Italian, American Comfort food, Asian Fusion, Vegetarian options, and even Desserts! You're sure to find something to satiate your craving.

  • How quickly can I expect my delivery?

    With DelishDrops, fast delivery is our priority. However, delivery times can vary depending on the restaurant, your location, and current traffic conditions. Generally, you can expect your meal to be delivered within 45 minutes of placing your order.

  • Can I track my order?

    Yes, once you place an order with DelishDrops, you can track your delivery until it arrives at your doorstep. This way you're always in the loop.

  • What are the payment options?

    DelishDrops accepts many forms of payment to make your order as easy as possible. You can pay through Credit/Debit Card, PayPal, or Cash on Delivery.

  • How can I contact DelishDrops for any queries?

    We're more than happy to assist! Please reach out to us through our "Contact Us" page for any queries, issues or suggestions. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
