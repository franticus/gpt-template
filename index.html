F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the duration of each class?

    Each TasteTango class is approximately 2 hours long. However, some advanced or specialty classes might run longer.

  • Do attendees need to bring their own ingredients?

    No, all ingredients and equipment for the class will be provided by TasteTango. All you need to bring is your enthusiasm for cooking!

  • Are the classes suitable for complete beginners?

    Yes, we offer a range of classes that cater to different skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned professionals.

  • Is it possible to host a private cooking event with TasteTango?

    Yes, TasteTango is more than happy to host private events. Whether it be team building for your organization or a birthday celebration, our team can create a custom culinary experience that fits your needs.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    All cancellations/rescheduling requests must be received at least 48 hours prior to your event. Any changes made within 48 hours will be subject to a charge unless you are rescheduling.

  • Can I book a class as a gift for someone else?

    Absolutely! Cooking classes make excellent gifts for a variety of occasions. You can purchase a class and give us their information at check out in the 'Order Note' section.
