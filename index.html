    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I book a session with FlashVisions?

    You can schedule a photography session directly from our website. Browse our service options, select a suitable time slot, and proceed to checkout.

    Do you offer services internationally?

    Yes, we do. We are keen to travel for projects and have already covered events in various locations across the globe.

    Do you provide a portfolio or sample work?

    Yes, indeed. Our portfolio is available on the website for you to gauge our style and quality of work.

    What if I’m not satisfied with the photos?

    Your satisfaction is important to us. If you're not pleased with the session or end results, please get in touch with us right away. We're committed to ensuring our clients' needs are fulfilled to the best of our abilities.

    Are FlashVisions services environmentally conscious?

    Yes, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint wherever possible. We practice energy efficient methods and strive to use digital processes as much as we can.

    Can I cancel or reschedule a photography session?

    Yes, we have a flexible cancellation and rescheduling policy. Please check our Conditions & Policy webpage for further details.
