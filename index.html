    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I order DreamGloss products?

    You can order our products directly from our website. Just browse the catalogue, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

    Do you ship internationally?

    Yes, we do. We offer international shipping to a majority of countries around the world.

    Do you offer samples or testers?

    Absolutely. We understand the importance of finding the right product for your skin, so we do offer samples and testers.

    What if a product causes skin irritation?

    If a product causes skin irritation, stop use immediately and contact us for assistance. Our customer service team is always ready to help.

    Are DreamGloss products cruelty-free?

    Yes, all DreamGloss cosmetics are cruelty-free and we are deeply committed to ethical sourcing and testing of ingredients.

    Can I return or exchange products?

    Yes, we offer a flexible return and exchange policy. Check our Returns & Exchanges webpage for further details.
