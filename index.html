Frequently Asked Questions

Renting a car can sometimes bring up a lot of questions - that's why we've compiled a list of FAQs to help you understand the ZoomCar process better.

  • Which types of cars does ZoomCar provide for renting?

    ZoomCar has a wide range of vehicles to suit your needs. From economy cars to luxury vehicles and SUVs, we have you covered. For specific models available, please refer to our fleet page or contact us.

  • How long in advance should I reserve a car?

    We recommend booking as early as possible, but we also cater to last-minute requests. Availability tends to decrease closer to the date, particularly during holiday periods.

  • Can I rent a car for someone else?

    While the reservation can be made by a different person, the individual listed as the driver on the rental agreement must present a valid driver's license at the time of rental.

  • Do you provide hourly/daily/weekly rentals?

    Yes, at ZoomCar we offer flexible rental solutions & options. You can rent hourly, for a few days, or even for several weeks to best fit your needs.

  • What policies do you have for fuel and mileage?

    Most of our rentals include unlimited mileage but this can vary for some special vehicles or offers. As for fuel, vehicles need to be returned with the same fuel level at which they were rented.
