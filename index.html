Awards And Recognitions

At Green Urban, we are proud of the recognition and appreciation that we have received from the industry, the media, and the community for our work in sustainable and eco-friendly urban development. Some of the awards and honors that we have achieved include:

The Green Building Award

This award is given by the Green Building Council of Sri Lanka to recognize the best practices and innovations in green building design and construction. We won this award for our project Green Urban City, a mixed-use development that features solar panels, rainwater harvesting, waste management, and green spaces.

The Urban Design Excellence Award

This award is given by the Urban Development Authority of Sri Lanka to recognize the excellence and creativity in urban design and planning. We won this award for our project Green Urban Park, a public park that integrates nature, culture, and recreation in a vibrant and accessible space.

The Best Real Estate Company of the Year

This award is given by the Sri Lanka Property Awards to recognize the outstanding performance and achievements of the real estate sector in Sri Lanka. We won this award for our overall excellence in delivering high-quality and affordable solutions for urban living that are environmentally friendly and socially responsible.
