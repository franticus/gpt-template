Frequently Asked Questions

For all your needs, questions, and concerns, we at InnerHarmony are here to provide enlightenment and guidance. Discover your true potential and achieve a balanced mental and emotional state with our effective and individualized strategies.

  • What can I expect in my first therapy session?

    Your initial session will include a comprehensive discussion about your concerns, feelings, and goals. Our seasoned therapists, led with a compassionate and understanding approach, will help you establish your therapy objectives.

  • Do you offer online therapy?

    Yes, we do offer online therapy. These sessions are as effective as the traditional in-person therapies, granting you the convenience of being attended to from the comfort of your home.

  • How long does each therapy session last?

    Each therapy session would typically last for about 50-60 minutes. However, the session duration might be adjusted depending upon your individual therapy plan and the nature of treatment required.

  • What if I need to cancel an appointment?

    We understand that unforeseen circumstances may occur. If you need to cancel your appointment, please do so 24 hours in advance. This gives us an opportunity to allocate the time slot to another client who might be in need.

  • Do you offer couples or family therapy?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive therapy services, including couples and family therapy. Our professional therapists can help in restoring the communication lines and strengthening relationships.
