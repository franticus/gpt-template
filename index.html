Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I request specific visual effects for my video?

    Absolutely! We encourage clients to share their vision and expectations with us. We do our best to incorporate desired visual effects, given that they coincide with our expertise and abilities.

  • Do you provide on-location shoots?

    Yes, we do! Cinemagic offers on-location shoots as part of our professional video production services. However, any additional costs related to location would need to be covered by the client.

  • How long does it typically take for a video project?

    The timeline varies with project complexity and length. However, a general rule is that a 5-minute video takes approximately 2-3 weeks from pre-production to final editing.

  • Is there any kind of video project you won't work on?

    We're open to most types of projects that allow us to express our creative ability. However, we retain the right to turn down projects that go against our ethical beliefs or company policies.

  • Do you provide a storyboard or script?

    Yes, we do provide a storyboard or script for videos. We believe working together with our clients to perfect the storyline and script is the first step to creating a cinematic masterpiece.

  • What if I need changes made after the final video is delivered?

    We offer a limited number of revisions based on the chosen package. Additional changes beyond the included revisions may incur extra charges. We always ensure our clients are happy with the final product.
