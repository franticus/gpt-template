F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How often should a standard home cleaning take place?

    Home cleaning is dependent on several factors like the size of the home and the amount of usage each area gets. Most homes settle well with bi-weekly cleanings, however, high-traffic homes or homes with pets might benefit from a weekly clean.

  • Do you provide all the cleaning supplies and equipment?

    Yes, at PureTidy, we provide all cleaning supplies and equipment. Our team uses eco-friendly products, ensuring no harmful chemicals are used in your space.

  • Are your services insured and your employees bonded?

    Yes, all of our services are insured and our employees are bonded. We take utmost care and responsibility for our work. In the rare event of an accident, you can be assured that it will be addressed properly.

  • Do you clean commercial spaces in addition to residential?

    Yes, at PureTidy we provide services for both residential and commercial cleaning. Our cleaning solutions are versatile and tailored to your space and needs.

  • What’s your cancellation policy?

    We request that you cancel at least 24 hours before a scheduled service. This gives us the opportunity to fill the schedule slot with another client.

  • Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

    No, you don't necessarily need to be at home while we clean. You just have to provide us a way to enter your house. Many of our clients prefer to give us a key, garage code or hide a key somewhere secure.
