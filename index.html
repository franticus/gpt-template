Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I get a custom nail design?

    Of course! At AcrylicNails, we love when our clients share their ideas and designs. Bring any design inspiration and we'll strive to make it come to life.

  • Do you offer home service?

    Unfortunately, we currently don't offer home services. However, our comfortable studio has been designed to make you feel like home while you get your nails done!

  • How long should I wait between appointments?

    That varies depending on your nail growth and maintenance. However, most clients come in for retouches every 2-3 weeks.

  • What type of nail products do you use?

    We use only professional-grade nail products from reputable brands. Furthermore, our products are carefully chosen to ensure that they're safe and cruelty-free.

  • What are the hygiene practices at AcrylicNails?

    AcrylicNails takes hygiene very seriously. All our tools are cleaned and sterilized between each appointment. We also maintain a clean studio environment to ensure your health and comfort.

  • Can I book an appointment for a group?

    Absolutely. We welcome group bookings for celebrations, bridal parties, or just a leisurely girls' day. Please get in touch with us in advance to ensure we can accommodate everyone.
