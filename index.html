F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does each training session take?

    Typically, each training session lasts about 60-75 minutes. However, this can vary depending on the individual's fitness level and the specifics of the training plan.

  • Can I cancel a booked session?

    Yes, you can cancel your booking. We request you to do so at least 24 hours in advance. You can read our Cancellation Policy for more information.

  • What makes your training techniques effective?

    At TrainXpert, our strategies are research-backed and tailored to individual needs, ensuring optimal results. We focus on holistic health improvements including strength, flexibility, endurance, and nutrition.

  • Do you provide a meal plan?

    Yes, we provide personalized meal plans tailored to align with your fitness goals. They are designed to ensure you have the right nutritional intake for optimal health and performance.

  • I have a health condition. Can I still sign up?

    Absolutely, we accommodate for any health issues. Our programs are customized to fit your specific needs and health condition. We recommend consulting with a health professional before starting any new fitness program.

  • I am a beginner. Do you offer plans suitable for me?

    Absolutely! We offer programs for all fitness levels. We can design a plan suitable for beginners to help you start your fitness journey effectively.
