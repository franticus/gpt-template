Frequently Asked Questions

With so much to plan, you might have questions about our process, payments, and more. Don't worry, we have compiled the most common queries about WhimsyNight in our FAQ section.

  • What services do you offer at WhimsyNight?

    At WhimsyNight, we provide comprehensive event planning services including but not limited to venue selection and booking, décor, catering, entertainment management, invitations, and transportation arrangements. Whether you're planning a small private gathering or a large corporate event, we're here to ensure your event is a success.

  • How far in advance should I book your services?

    The earlier, the better. We often book events 3 to 4 months ahead, especially for peak seasons. However, we always try our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

  • Do you handle destination events?

    Yes, our logistics team specializes in destination events and we can handle logistics to ensure your event is seamless, even in a new location.

  • Do you provide any packages for events?

    Yes, we do have event packages to cater to different requirements and budgets. However, each event is unique, and we can customize our services to meet your specific needs.

  • Who will be my point of contact throughout the planning process?

    Once you choose to work with us, you will be assigned a dedicated event manager who will be your primary point of contact throughout the planning process. They will ensure your vision is executed to the highest standard.
