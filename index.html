    Modifying your workshop choice is allowed within a specific time frame. Please refer to our policy for detailed information on workshop changes.

    How can I track the progress of my craft learning journey?

    Right after enrolling, you will receive access to a dedicated learning portal where you can easily monitor your progress and access course materials.

    Are there any special requirements for certain craft workshops?

    Some craft workshops may have specific prerequisites or materials needed. Review the workshop guidelines for precise instructions before registering for a class.

    What kinds of support options are available for learners?

    We offer various support channels and resources for our learners. Please consult our support policy for details on available assistance and associated resources.

    What should I do if I encounter challenges in my craft learning journey?

    In case of difficulties or questions, please reach out to our learner support team for assistance. We are dedicated to helping you overcome any challenges in your craft learning experience.
