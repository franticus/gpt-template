F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does delivery take?

    Our standard delivery takes around 5-7 business days. However, we also offer express shipping which will get your order to you in 1-2 business days.

  • Do you accept returns?

    Yes, we do. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please return the item within 14 days of receiving it in its original condition. Please refer to our Return Policy for more details.

  • Where do you source your vintage items from?

    Our vintage items are sourced globally - from estate sales, auctions, and directly from collectors. We ensure that every piece is genuine and authentic.

  • Do you provide information on how to care for vintage items?

    Absolutely! We provide care instructions specific to each item purchased. Proper care will ensure you enjoy your vintage pieces for years to come.

  • Can I reserve an item for future purchase?

    Unfortunately, due to the uniqueness of our inventory, we are unable to reserve items.

  • Can I purchase a gift card for someone else?

    Absolutely! Our gift cards make the perfect present for the vintage lovers in your life. Just head to the 'Gift Card' section on our site to purchase.
