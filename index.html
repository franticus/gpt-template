Frequently Asked Questions

At GlideWheels, we're here to assist with any inquiries or needs you might have. We're dedicated to delivering the best of urban mobility, tailored just for you.

  • What types of bikes and scooters do you offer?

    We offer a comprehensive range of bikes and scooters suitable for all terrain and skill levels. Our equipment includes models from renowned international manufacturers.

  • Do you provide delivery and pick-up options?

    Yes, we do provide delivery and pick-up services for our products. Please contact us for detailed information and specifics on our delivery policies.

  • What are your operating hours?

    Our online service operates 24/7, enabling you to place orders at your convenience. Our physical operation and customer service is available Monday to Sunday, from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

  • Do you offer equipment for different skill levels?

    Yes, we do. We offer bikes and scooters that cater to beginners, intermediate enthusiasts, and professional riders, allowing everyone to enjoy a seamless ride.

  • How do I choose the right vehicle for my needs?

    Our support team is always ready to guide you in making the best choice for your mobility needs. Simply reach out to us with your requirements and preferences, and we'll take it from there.
