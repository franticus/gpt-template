Frequently Asked Questions

We realize that you may have questions about our unique fashion products at DistinctAttire. We have gathered some common inquiries to help you navigate our offerings.

  • What types of clothing do you offer?

    DistinctAttire offers an assortment of fashion-forward clothing, including dresses, suits, casual wear, and accessories. If you crave unique style, you'll find something here.

  • How do you ensure the quality of your products?

    We pride ourselves on quality. All our products are crafted by skilled tailors using top-tier materials, ensuring that you get a piece that not only looks good but will also last.

  • What is your return & exchange policy?

    We have a customer-friendly return and exchange policy. If an item doesn't fit your expectations, you can return or exchange it within 14 days of receiving your order.

  • How much does shipping cost and how long does it take?

    Shipping costs vary depending on your location and the size of the order, but we always strive to find the best courier rates. Generally, we aim to have your order at your doorstep within 5-7 business days.

  • Do you have offline stores, or solely operate online?

    At the moment, we operate as an online-first brand. This model allows us to cater to our customers nationwide and keep our prices competitive without compromising on the quality of our unique attire.
