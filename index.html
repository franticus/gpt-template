Step 1: Consultation and Design

Start by scheduling a consultation at Enigma Tattoo Salon. Share your ideas, preferences, and any specific elements you want in your tattoo. Collaborate with the tattoo artist to create a custom design that reflects your vision.

Step 2: Booking and Preparation

Once the design is finalized, book your tattoo session at Enigma Tattoo Salon. Confirm the date, time, and payment details. Prior to your appointment, follow any necessary preparation instructions provided by the salon, such as avoiding certain medications or alcohol.

Step 3: Tattoo Session and Aftercare

On the scheduled day, arrive at the salon for your tattoo session. The skilled artist will carefully ink your design onto your skin. Afterward, they will provide you with detailed aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing and maintenance of your tattoo.
