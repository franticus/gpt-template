About us

The largest sports gaming platform in the world, Cric Gamer Fantasy App, is used by over a million users to play Fantasy Cricket. It offers a fantasy sports management platform where sports fans can share their knowledge of the game. Users of the game will have the opportunity to compete against other players and earn points by creating their own teams based on real-life players in upcoming matches.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • What is Cric Gamer Fantasy Cricket?

    In India, cricket is more than just a sport; it is a religion. On Cric Gamer, you can participate in contests for every format of international cricket. Users can also play online cricket games from various cricket leagues around the world, including the IPL, apart from ICC matches and tournaments. What is the procedure for joining and playing a fantasy cricket game on Cric Gamer? Your email address and mobile number are all you need to register. There are rules in the game that ensure #FairPlay for all players.

  • How do I sign up on Cric Gamer?

    Easy breezy! Getting verified through OTP on our Fantasy Sports Platform is easy. Once you’re on Cric Gamer, fill out the short form to start playing.

  • On Cric Gamer, how many accounts can I create with the same email address?

    There can only be one account created per email address on Cric Gamer. Our Fair Play Policy prohibits users from creating more than one account on Cric Gamer.

  • How safe is it to add money to Cric Gamer?

    The process of adding money to your Cric Gamer account is both simple and safe. With Cric Gamer, you can choose from many different payment options to ensure that your personal information is safe. And what’s more? Cric Gamer allows you to withdraw your winnings directly to your bank account after you verify your personal information.

  • What is the process for receiving my winnings?

    IMPS withdrawals should be credited within three working days or almost immediately. Within three working days, NEFT should be credited.

  • Why can’t I log in to my Cric Gamer account?

    Sorry for the inconvenience! Make sure you’ve entered the correct mobile number and password, and then try again. Please click on “Forgot Password” and enter your registered number on Cricket Fantasy. We will immediately send you an OTP to your registered number to reset your password. You can play fantasy sports online on Cricket Fantasy if you follow the instructions given.
