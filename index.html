Frequently Asked Questions

For all your inquiries and requirements, we at GreenAcres are here to provide clarification and assistance. Enjoy the bounty of nature in its purest form with our tree-to-table approach, aimed at delivering health and happiness.

  • What type of produce can I expect in my box?

    Each box is curated to include a variety of locally-sourced, seasonally available fruits and vegetables. We also offer options to add fresh bread, handmade cheese, and other farm-fresh products.

  • Can I customize my box?

    Yes, we provide the flexibility to customize your box based on your dietary preferences and needs. You can choose from our add-ons and compliment your box with your preferred items.

  • What are the delivery days and time slots?

    We deliver throughout the week except on Sundays. Once you place an order, you can select your preferred delivery slot. We strive to ensure that your produce reaches you as quickly as possible.

  • How do I store my produce to ensure they stay fresh?

    All of our produce is harvested at its peak and delivered to you as quickly as possible. To retain freshness, most produce should be stored in a cool, dry place or in the refrigerator. Specific storage tips are provided with your delivery.

  • Do you use pesticides on the plants?

    We're committed to sustainable farming practices. This means we aim to use organic, botanical, or mineral-derived pesticides whenever possible. Any non-organic pesticide use is minimal and only done when absolutely necessary.
