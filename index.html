Frequently Asked Questions

What type of warranty is included with my purchase?

PCZone provides a standard one-year warranty for all devices. Extended warranties can also be purchased during the checkout phase. This covers factory defects but does not include damages caused by misuse.

Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases?

Yes, PCZone offers volume pricing for businesses and organizations that need to purchase in bulk. Please contact us for more information regarding bulk purchases.

Can I get a customized PC build?

Absolutely! We offer custom PC builds for our Pro customers. Our team works personally with you to select and assemble components that suit your unique needs.

What methods of payment do you accept?

PCZone accepts several payment methods including Visa, MasterCard, American Express credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Please let us know if you need to use a different form of payment.
