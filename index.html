Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are your seeds suitable for all climates?

    We offer a variety of seeds suitable for different climate zones. Information regarding the suitable climate is indicated on each seed package.

  • Are your garden tools covered by a warranty?

    Yes, all our gardening tools are covered by a 1-year warranty. This covers manufacture defects and normal wear and tear.

  • Does your garden furniture require assembly?

    Some of our garden furniture will require assembly. We provide an instruction manual and all necessary tools with these products for self-assembly.

  • What does your landscape service include?

    Our landscape service includes everything from initial consultation and design, through plant and materials selection, to the installation process. We also offer regular maintenance for complex projects.

  • Can I return a product if I'm not satisfied?

    Yes, we maintain a 30-day return policy, during which a customer can return the purchased item if they are not satisfied. For more information, please refer to our return policy page.

  • Do you deliver to all regions?

    We aim to deliver to every region possible. However, please do get in touch with us if you are unsure so we can confirm if delivery is possible.
