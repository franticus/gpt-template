    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I rent an office space?

    You can start by contacting our customer service team who will guide you through the process. We require proof of business or organization registration and a signed rental agreement.

    What are the rental period options?

    Our rental contracts range from a period of six months to several years, according to your business needs.

    Can I tour the office spaces before renting?

    Absolutely. We encourage all prospective tenants to schedule a tour of our office spaces before making a decision.

    What should I do in case of maintenance issues?

    Our property management team is on-site and ready to address any maintenance issues that may arise.

    Are the office spaces furnished?

    We offer both furnished and unfurnished office spaces. Our furnished offices are fully equipped and move-in ready, while our unfurnished spaces provide the perfect canvas for your business.

    Do you provide parking spaces?

    Yes, we offer secure parking facilities for our tenants and their guests at a competitive rate.
