Frequently Asked Questions

At BottleBliss, we're here to help with any inquiries or requirements you might have. We're dedicated to bringing you the very best of the world's wines and spirits, right to your doorstep.

  • What types of wines and spirits do you sell?

    We offer a wide range of both wines and spirits. Our collection includes bottles from all major wine producing regions and some of the world's top distilleries for spirits.

  • Do you provide delivery internationally?

    Yes, we do provide international shipping for certain products. Please get in touch with us for detailed information and specifics on shipping policies.

  • What are your operational hours?

    Our online store operates 24/7, so you can place your order at any time that is convenient for you. Our customer service is available Monday to Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

  • How do I store my wines and spirits to ensure they stay fresh?

    Storage can greatly influence the taste and longevity of your bottles. As a rule of thumb, store your wines horizontally in a cool, dark, and slightly humid place. Spirits should be stored upright to avoid cork damage, in a place without direct sunlight or drastic temperature changes.

  • How do I know which wine or spirit to choose for my event?

    We're always here to assist you in such a decision. You can contact us and provide the details of your event, and we'll guide you towards the best choices based on your preferences and budget.
