Frequently Asked Questions

Head over to our FAQs section for answers to commonly asked queries about LearnLingo, the courses, duration, assessments, and much more.

  • What is the course structure at LearnLingo?

    Each course at LearnLingo is divided into modules and further into lessons, giving learners a step-by-step approach to mastering a language fully and efficiently. We include a mixture of reading, writing, listening, and speaking exercises.

  • How long does a typical course take to complete?

    Each course duration varies according to the language and your learning pace. You can learn at your own pace and choose the frequency and duration of learning per session.

  • Is there a test or certification upon course completion?

    Yes, at the end of every course, there is an assessment test to evaluate your comprehension of the language. After passing the test, you will receive a certification of completion.

  • What languages can I learn with LearnLingo?

    LearnLingo offers a diverse range of languages including but not limited to Spanish, French, German, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and Russian. We are always adding new languages based on demand.

  • Do I have to pay for each course individually?

    No, we offer a subscription-based model which allows you unlimited access to all the courses and features on our platform.
