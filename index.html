    FAQ

    Answers to Common Questions

    Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

    Modifying your delivery address is possible within a specified time frame. Refer to our policy for detailed information on address changes.

    How can I track the status of my courier delivery?

    Right after placing your order, you will receive access to a dedicated tracking platform where you can easily monitor the status and location of your courier delivery.

    Do I need any special packaging for fragile items?

    Special packaging requirements may apply to fragile items. Review the shipping guidelines for specific instructions before booking a courier service.

    What types of insurance options are available for my deliveries?

    We offer a variety of insurance coverage options for your deliveries. Please refer to our insurance policy for details on coverage plans and associated costs.

    What should I do if my courier package is delayed?

    In case of delivery delays, please reach out to our customer support for assistance. We will promptly investigate and resolve any issues related to delayed courier packages.
