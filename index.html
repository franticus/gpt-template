Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to our FAQ section to ease your doubts regarding our landscaping services. Here, you'll find answers to commonly asked questions regarding our design process, cost, plant selection, maintenance, and more.

  • What does the landscape design process involve?

    The process involves consultation, site evaluation, conceptual design, plant and material selection, and project installation. We also provide post-installation care guides to maintain the look of your landscape.

  • How long does a typical project take?

    The duration of the project relies on the project's size, complexity, and the efficiencies of third-party contractors. However, we make sure to complete the task without compromising on the quality of work.

  • Do you provide maintenance services?

    Yes, apart from design and installation, we also offer maintenance services to help preserve the beauty of your landscape.

  • What is included in the costs?

    The costs include design fees, installation, materials, plants, and any required prep work such as soil testing or land grading.

  • Do you offer customized landscaping designs?

    Absolutely! All our designs are tailor-made to suit the individual needs, preferences, and living conditions of our clients.
