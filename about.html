About Mobiliaris

Our Expertise

At Mobiliaris, we take immense pride in presenting an expertise that is crafted over years of dedicated work and unyielding passion for design and artisanship. Our team, consisting of expert designers, meticulous carpenters, and dedicated customer service specialists, brings together a diverse set of skills and knowledge critical to elevate the standards of what's traditionally expected of custom furniture. We don't merely create a piece of furniture; we believe in creating bespoke creations that emotionally resonate with our customers, augmenting their space, both aesthetically and functionally. Our commitment to delivering unparalleled craftsmanship combined with our vision to drive innovation is at the heart of what we do.

Craftsmanship & Innovation

Across decades, we have held the art of woodworking very dear to our hearts. This time-honored tradition forms the core of our construction techniques. But we've not let our deep-rooted respect for tradition railroad us into obscurity. Moving with the times, we've successfully paired these traditional techniques with the contemporary and avant-garde trends in design. Consequently, every piece of furniture curated by our team is not merely a functional addition to your space. Instead, it is a symbol of a harmonious blend of trend and tradition, catching the eye while adding to the aesthetics of your room.

We're not just creators; we're also preservers of memory and legacy. Furniture, which has been part of homes and offices for years, can deteriorate over time. However, they often hold great sentimental value, and discarding them would be unbearable. That's where our furniture restoration expertise comes into the picture. With a combined skill set of refurbishing and restoring, we breathe life back into your much-loved pieces, reinforcing their strength while maintaining their visual appeal.

Our strength lies not only in our hands-on skills but also in our interaction with our clients. We understand that excellent customer service forms the backbone of any thriving enterprise. This understanding motivates us to handle each customer query with great care, ensuring that we deliver not only top-tier furniture solutions but also a smooth and enjoyable customer journey from the moment you connect with us.

Our expertise in furniture design and woodworking is quantifiable, but we believe the actual measure of our success lies in the satisfaction and joy our work brings to our clients' lives. Cookie settings

Cookies are small pieces of data sent from a website and stored on the user's computer by the web browser while it is running. The browser stores each message in a small file called a cookie. When you request another page from the server, your browser sends the cookie back to the server. Cookies have been developed as a reliable mechanism that allows websites to remember information or record user activity when browsing pages.

  Necessary ∨

Strictly necessary cookies - usually these cookies are necessary for the operation of the site. As a rule, without these cookies, the operation of the site is not possible or its functioning may be severely impaired.

  Functional ∨

Functional cookies - usually these cookies are used to improve the functional characteristics of your site and make it easier for users to use. These cookies remember the settings selected by users (for example, language and time zone settings). With the help of these files, users can avoid changing the settings each time they visit the site.

  Statistics ∨

Performance cookies - usually these cookies show whether the user has visited your site before. Analytical cookies allow you to recognize and count the number of users of your site and see how these users move around your site.

  Marketing ∨

Marketing, targeting, and advertising cookies – Usually, these cookies record visitors' visits to your site, the pages they visited, and the links they clicked on. This information is used to make the advertisements displayed on it more relevant to the interests of the user. Advertising cookies allow you to know if the user has already seen a specific advertisement or an advertisement of a certain type.
