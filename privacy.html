Privacy Policy

Welcome to Lustrum Photo Studio's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to safeguard your data. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We may update this policy from time to time, so please review it periodically. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, feel free to contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

Information Collection

At Lustrum Photo Studio, we collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. The information we gather includes:

  • Personal information, such as your name and contact details, provided voluntarily when you interact with us.
  • Device information, including your IP address and browser type, collected automatically when you access our website.
  • Usage data, such as the pages you visit and the actions you take on our platform.

Information Usage

We use the collected information for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services.
  • Personalizing your experience and delivering relevant content.
  • Analyzing and improving our website's performance and user experience.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • For the purpose of data processing by trusted third-party service providers.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Our commitment to your privacy extends beyond mere compliance with regulations. We believe in transparency and want you to understand how your information is handled. Rest assured that your trust is of utmost importance to Lustrum Photo Studio.

When you engage with our photo studio, we may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. These technologies help us understand user behavior, optimize our website, and provide you with tailored content. You have the option to manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Lustrum Photo Studio is dedicated to maintaining the accuracy of the information we collect. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you need assistance with these actions, please reach out to our dedicated support team.

As part of our ongoing efforts to improve, we may introduce new features or update existing ones. We will always strive to keep you informed about any changes that may impact your privacy. Regularly check our Privacy Policy for the latest information.

We understand the importance of protecting the privacy of children. Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to address the issue.

At Lustrum Photo Studio, we value the relationships we build with our clients. We may send you newsletters, updates, or promotional materials to keep you informed about our latest offerings. You can opt-out of receiving such communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

Our Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of third-party websites or services linked to or from our platform. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of those third parties, as we are not responsible for their practices.

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, we encourage you to contact us first. However, if you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

Lustrum Photo Studio is constantly striving to enhance its privacy practices. We appreciate your feedback and suggestions. Your input helps us refine our policies and ensures that we continue to meet the highest standards of data protection.
