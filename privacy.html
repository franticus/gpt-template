Privacy Policy

Welcome to Sculitur Fresh Market's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our fresh market.

Collection of Information

We collect information when you shop at our fresh market, subscribe to newsletters, or interact with our website. The information may include your name, contact details, and preferences. Rest assured, we only collect data relevant to enhancing your fresh shopping experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to personalize your shopping journey, improve our services, and keep you informed about exciting fresh opportunities. Your data is treated with the utmost confidentiality and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Security Measures

We employ advanced security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Our commitment is to ensure that your information is safe and secure throughout your association with Sculitur Fresh Market.

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission.
  • Regular security audits and updates to our systems.
  • Restricted access to personal information, limited to authorized personnel.

Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us analyze website traffic and optimize your interaction with our content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Choices

We respect your right to control your personal information. You have the option to update or delete your data from our records. If you wish to make changes, please reach out to our dedicated support team.

Marketing Communications

When you subscribe to our newsletters, you may receive occasional updates about our fresh produce and events. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in the emails.

International Users

If you are accessing our website or services from outside the United States, be aware that your information may be processed in the United States or other countries with different data protection laws. Rest assured, we take steps to ensure your data is handled securely and in accordance with applicable laws.

  • Implementation of the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield framework.
  • Ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
  • Regular reviews of our data processing practices to align with international standards.

Links to External Sites

Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. We have no control over the content and practices of these sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Policy Effectiveness

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the latest update and governs the use of our services. Continued use of our website and services implies acceptance of this policy.

Data Subject Rights

As a user, you have rights concerning your personal information. These rights may include the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us.

Commitment to Data Security

We are dedicated to protecting your data from unauthorized access. Our security measures include encryption, access controls, and regular assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Updates to Our Practices

Our commitment to your privacy extends to ongoing improvements in our data protection practices. We regularly review and update our processes to align with industry standards and legal requirements.

Contact Us

For any inquiries or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@sculitur.com. We value your privacy and are here to address any questions you may have.
