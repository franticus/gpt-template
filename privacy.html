4. Cookie Policy

OldSoulStyle uses "Cookies," along with other technologies and third-party services, to personalize retro fashion shopping experiences, deliver relevant product recommendations, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is a piece of data sent from the site to the visitor's device, which is then sent back by the visitor's device each time they return to the site. Cookies help us better understand customer behavior, track product interests, and remember site preferences. Users who have concerns about cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing OldSoulStyle's vintage fashion shopping services. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.

Continuing to use our vintage fashion shopping services without changing your cookie settings signifies your agreement to OldSoulStyle's use of cookies.
