Welcome to Ligyting Language Hub's Privacy Policy

At Ligyting, we are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data as you embark on your language learning journey with us.

Information We Collect: We may collect personal information, including but not limited to, your name, email address, and language preferences. This data helps us tailor our language courses to your individual needs and provide a personalized learning experience.

How We Use Your Information: Your data is used to enhance your learning experience, personalize content, and communicate important updates. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent unless required by law.

Your Privacy, Our Priority

Data Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Our commitment is to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Cookie Policy: To enhance user experience, we use cookies. These small text files are stored on your device to improve site functionality. You have the option to manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy: Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When your data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized to ensure your privacy.

Third-Party Links: Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

International Transfers: If you are accessing our platform from outside the United States, be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our servers are located. Your use of our services constitutes consent to this transfer.

Policy Updates: We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the last update will be indicated. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

User Responsibilities: While we take all necessary measures to secure your data, your active participation in protecting your information is crucial. Ensure the confidentiality of your login credentials and contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information if required by law, regulation, or legal process. This includes responding to lawful requests from public authorities to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

User Consent: By using our language learning platform, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any aspect of this policy, please discontinue use of our services and contact us with any concerns.

Your Choices, Your Control

  • Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us. You can do so by following the instructions provided in our emails.
  • Access and Update: You can review and update your personal information by logging into your account on our platform.
  • Deactivate Account: If you wish to deactivate your account, please contact us at info@madrahon.com.

Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy or our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@madrahon.com. We are here to ensure your language learning experience is not only enriching but also secure and private.
