Privacy

At Sculitur Fresh Market, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and your choices regarding their usage.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, including recognizing you on subsequent visits, remembering your preferences, and improving site performance. Cookies also help us analyze how users interact with our site.

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the proper functioning of our website. They enable core features such as security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our site, such as which pages they visit most frequently. This data helps us optimize our website for better performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our site to remember your choices, such as language preferences or region, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: We use these cookies to deliver relevant ads to you based on your interests. They also limit the number of times you see an ad and help measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to choose whether to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may affect your experience on our website.

To manage your cookie preferences, refer to the settings in your browser. You can also use third-party tools and browser extensions to control cookie behavior.

Changes to Our Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, so please review it periodically for the latest information.

Cookie Usage Transparency: We believe in transparent communication about how we use cookies. This policy aims to provide you with clear insights into the types and purposes of cookies employed on our platform.

Third-Party Cookies: In addition to our own cookies, we may also use third-party cookies from trusted partners. These cookies help us track advertising effectiveness and enhance the overall user experience.

Managing Your Preferences

Opting Out: If you wish to opt out of targeted advertising, many third-party ad networks offer options to do so. You can also use browser settings to disable certain types of cookies.

Consent Renewal: Periodically, we may ask you to review and renew your consent for cookie usage. This ensures that you have control over your privacy preferences and are aware of any changes.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Data Security: We take the security of your data seriously. Our use of cookies is designed to enhance your experience without compromising your privacy or the security of your personal information.

Continuous Improvement: We are committed to continuous improvement in our use of cookies. Your feedback is valuable in helping us refine our practices and provide a seamless online experience.

Stay Informed

Policy Updates: We recommend checking this Cookie Policy periodically for any updates. We will inform you of any significant changes and provide an overview of how these changes may impact your cookie choices.
