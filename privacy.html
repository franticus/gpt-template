Privacy Policy

Welcome to Noftebok Real Estate's Privacy Policy. This document outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy as you engage with our real estate services.

Your privacy is crucial to us, and we strive to be transparent about the information we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using our platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect:

  • We collect personal information, including your name, contact details, and preferences, to provide you with tailored real estate services.
  • Non-personal information, such as your browsing behavior and interactions with our platform, helps us improve our services and user experience.
  • We may gather information from third-party sources to enhance the accuracy and completeness of our real estate offerings.

Your Privacy Choices

At Noftebok Real Estate, we respect your privacy choices. You have the right to:

  • Access and control the personal information we collect about you.
  • Update and correct inaccuracies in your information.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications.

How We Use Your Information:

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and improve our real estate services.
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences.
  • Communicate important updates and relevant property listings.

Security Measures

Your security is our priority. We employ industry-standard measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Information Sharing:

We may share your information with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in delivering quality real estate services. Rest assured, we ensure that these entities uphold the same privacy standards we commit to.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These small text files are stored on your device and help us analyze user behavior, improve our site's functionality, and provide personalized content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy:

Noftebok Real Estate does not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly address the situation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly for any updates. Your continued use of our services after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the changes.

International Data Transfers:

If you access our real estate services from outside the United States, be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. or other locations. By using our platform, you consent to these transfers.

Third-Party Links:

Our real estate platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any linked platforms.

Data Retention:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

California Privacy Rights:

California residents have the right to request information regarding the disclosure of their personal information to third parties for direct marketing purposes. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact us.

GDPR Compliance:

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). To learn more about these rights and how to exercise them, please contact us.

Effective Date:

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date indicated at the top of the document. Any updates or changes will be reflected with a revised effective date.

We regularly review and enhance our security practices to adapt to evolving threats and maintain a secure real estate environment for our users.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@noftebok.com. We value your privacy and are here to address any inquiries you may have.
