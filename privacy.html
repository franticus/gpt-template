Privacy Policy

Welcome to Ambientia – your trusted partner in creating exquisite interior designs that reflect your unique style and preferences.

At Ambientia, we prioritize your privacy, ensuring a secure and enjoyable experience while exploring our innovative design solutions. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

By engaging with Ambientia’s services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Details: We may collect your name, contact information, and other details when you interact with us for design consultations or inquiries.

2. Site Usage: Ambientia utilizes cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze trends, and gather information about user interactions on our website.

3. Design Preferences: To provide personalized design solutions, we may collect information about your interior design preferences, style, and project requirements.

How We Use Your Information

  • We use your personal information to communicate with you, respond to inquiries, and provide tailored design recommendations.
  • Aggregate data helps us analyze user behavior, improve our services, and enhance the functionality of our website.
  • Your design preferences guide us in creating bespoke interior solutions that align with your vision.

Security Measures

At Ambientia, we employ robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our commitment is to maintain the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

We utilize industry-standard encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits to ensure the highest level of protection for your personal information.

Your trust in Ambientia is paramount, and we take every measure to uphold the privacy and security of your data.

Your Choices

1. Opt-Out: You have the option to opt-out of receiving marketing communications from Ambientia.

2. Cookie Preferences: Adjust your browser settings to control cookie usage on our website, though this may impact certain features.

3. Access and Update: You can request access to your personal information and update any inaccuracies through our designated channels.

4. Third-Party Links: Our website may contain links to third-party websites for additional design inspiration. Be aware that these external sites have their privacy policies, and Ambientia holds no responsibility for their practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

5. Children’s Privacy: Ambientia’s services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Retention: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you wish to have your information removed from our records, please contact us, and we will promptly address your request.

7. Changes to Policy: Ambientia may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

8. International Transfers: Your information may be transferred and stored on servers located outside your country. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your data to locations that may have different data protection laws.

9. Consent: By engaging with Ambientia, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy. Any changes to our policies will be communicated through our website or other appropriate channels.

10. Governing Law: This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of [Your Country]. Any disputes arising from the use of our services will be subject to the jurisdiction of the courts in [Your Jurisdiction].

11. Customer Feedback: We value your feedback on our design solutions and services. Your reviews and testimonials contribute to our continuous improvement and inspire others to embark on their design journey with Ambientia. Share your experiences with us through our dedicated feedback channels.

12. Accessibility Commitment: Ambientia is committed to making our design solutions accessible to everyone. If you encounter accessibility barriers or have specific accommodation requests, please reach out to us. We strive to ensure a seamless and inclusive experience for all.
