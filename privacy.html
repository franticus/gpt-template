Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At CricWinnerTop, we offer a fantasy sports management platform that allows sports enthusiasts to engage with the game like never before. We respect the privacy of our Users. Our Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from our Users, how we manage this information, and the choices available to our Users concerning these details.

Our Privacy Policy is in line with our Website's Terms of Service. CricWinnerTop respects your privacy and ensures that any information provided by you is securely protected according to this Privacy Policy. To participate in building and competing with fantasy teams on CricWinnerTop, we may require specific information from you.

Our Information Collection Process

Users must register to create fantasy teams and use our services. The personal information we collect includes names, addresses, email addresses, and phone numbers. If the necessary information is not provided, access to our services may be restricted. In addition, we collect non-personal information such as your IP address, which is used solely for analytical purposes, managing the Website, tracking Users' activity, and compiling aggregated demographic information. CricWinnerTop exclusively owns the information collected on this website and pledges not to sell, share, or rent this information except as detailed in this Privacy Policy.

How We Utilize Collected Information

CricWinnerTop does not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except for scenarios where we might need to do so under legal requirements. By registering on our platform, you permit us to utilize and disclose your personal information as set out in this document. Personal information is used to enhance service quality, user experience, and to communicate about new offers, products, and services related to CricWinnerTop. Select information may be shared with partners and third parties that facilitate our rewards and offerings system.

A robust security system is implemented at CricWinnerTop to prevent loss, misuse, and unauthorized alterations of User information. Your account and registration information are password-protected, ensuring that only you can access this personal data. CricWinnerTop is not responsible for any unauthorized account activities that occur under your password. We strongly recommend that you do not share your password with anyone.

Cookies

CricWinnerTop may store cookies on your device during your visit. Cookies are small text files that serve to distinguish your browser uniquely. They facilitate personalizing your experience and may be used for authentication and security. Cookies do not allow us to access any personal information beyond what you choose to share.

Advertisers on CricWinnerTop may also use cookies when you engage with their content. In these cases, the cookies are managed by the advertisers, not by us.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser's settings to decline cookies if you prefer, which may affect your overall user experience.

User Consent

By registering on our Website or App, you provide explicit consent for CricWinnerTop to collect and use your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We may update our practices and this Privacy Policy periodically. Such changes will be updated on this page, so we encourage you to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

CricWinnerTop emphasizes that this policy is a statement of our customary operations concerning User information, not a contractual promise or legal claim. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time without prior notice.
