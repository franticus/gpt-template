4. Cookie Policy

Spartanum Gym uses "Cookies," along with other technologies and third-party services, to personalize workout experiences, deliver relevant program recommendations, and remember user preferences.

A cookie is a piece of data sent from the site to the visitor's device, which is then sent back by the visitor's device each time they return to the site. Cookies help us better understand member behavior, track program interests, and remember site preferences. Users who have concerns about cookies should adjust their browser settings before accessing Spartanum Gym's fitness services. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.

Continuing to use our fitness services without changing your cookie settings signifies your agreement to Spartanum Gym's use of cookies.
