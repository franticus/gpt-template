Privacy Policy

Welcome to Handybok Travel Agency's Privacy Policy. At Handybok, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us.

Data Collection and Usage: We collect various types of information to enhance your user experience. This may include personal details, browsing history, and preferences. Rest assured that we use this data solely for improving our services and tailoring them to your needs.

Information Sharing: Handybok does not sell or share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. We respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your data.

Your Privacy Rights

Handybok acknowledges and respects your privacy rights. You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. If you have any inquiries or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us through the provided channels.

Security Measures: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our secure systems ensure that your information is treated with the utmost confidentiality.

Cookies and Tracking: Handybok uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies are designed to improve site functionality and provide a personalized experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Handybok does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action.

Contact Us: For any inquiries or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at info@handybok.com. Your privacy is our priority, and we are dedicated to addressing your questions promptly.

Data Retention

Handybok retains your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We regularly review our data retention practices to ensure compliance with regulations.

Changes to Privacy Policy: Handybok reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

External Links: Our website may contain links to external sites. Handybok is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites. We encourage users to be aware when leaving our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personal information.

GDPR Compliance: Handybok is committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for individuals within the European Union. If you are accessing our services from the EU, you have specific rights under the GDPR, and we ensure these rights are respected.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA): California residents have additional privacy rights under the CCPA. Handybok respects and complies with these rights, including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information.
